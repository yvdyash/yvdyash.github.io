Background

Apart from my interest in software development and distributed systems, I am a Math & Competitive Programming enthusiast. I love solving algorithmic problems and you can find me on Codeforces and Codechef as 'yvdyash'. During my undergraduate program, I have been the Programming Head of the PICT ACM Student Chapter and headed multiple programming event teams for one of the biggest annual Techfests, Pulzion organized by PASC. I oversaw Problem setting, testing and infrastructure for the same.

Alongside software development, I have worked on building applications which solved some interesting problems making use of Machine Learning, Computer Vision, CNNs and Transfer Learning. I secured a prize for the project 'Generation of Cricket Match Highlights Video' in Impetus and Concepts 2019 under the catergory - ML and Multimedia. I worked on another interesting project 'In-video searching System' as part of my 9 months internship at Persistent Systems in 2018.

While pursuing my M.S. Degree, I worked as a Teaching Assistant for the graduate course - CSE 551 Fundamentals of Algorithms, for the ASU Online MCS Degree where I was responsible for holding office hours, grading homework & exams and creating/maintaining online instructional content.

After completing a wonderful summer internship in 2022, I will be returning to eBay as a Software Engineer 2 in the San Jose office where I will be developing software for eBay Payments.
